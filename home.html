ABOUT LOREDANA

Hello! Thank you for stopping by! I’m Loredana, Professional Makeup Artist, located on Iasi. My passion for makeup artistry began at a very young age. I can remember going to my aunt Trini’s home and my cousin and I would make lotions and potions. My Aunt always reminded me of a movie star and it was her beauty and the products she used that inspired me to follow my dream.
